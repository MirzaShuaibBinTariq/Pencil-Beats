Chapter 4
Improvement in Food Resources

Food is one of the basic necessities of life. This chapter explains scientific methods used to increase food production through crop improvement, better farming practices, animal husbandry, fisheries, and apiculture. These methods help provide sufficient, nutritious, and high-quality food for a growing population.

Start Learning →

📚 What You Will Learn

This chapter explains modern techniques used to improve food resources. You will study crop improvement, nutrient management, irrigation, cropping patterns, crop protection, grain storage, animal husbandry, fish production, and beekeeping.

🌾 Crop Improvement

Learn scientific methods to increase crop yield and quality.

🚜 Crop Production

Understand irrigation, nutrient management and farming practices.

🌱 Cropping Patterns

Study mixed cropping, intercropping and crop rotation.

🛡 Crop Protection

Learn methods to protect crops from weeds, pests and diseases.

🌾 Grain Storage

Understand safe storage methods and prevention of grain losses.

🐄 Animal Husbandry

Learn scientific management of cattle, poultry and other domestic animals.

🐟 Fisheries & Apiculture

Study fish production and beekeeping as important food resources.

🎯 Learning Outcomes

After completing this chapter, you will be able to:

  • Explain the importance of improving food resources.
  • Describe different methods of crop variety improvement.
  • Understand nutrient management and irrigation practices.
  • Differentiate between mixed cropping, intercropping and crop rotation.
  • Explain methods of crop protection and grain storage.
  • Describe the principles of animal husbandry.
  • Explain cattle farming, poultry farming, fisheries and apiculture.
  • Solve JKBOSE examination questions confidently.

📝 Chapter Overview

Food is essential for the survival of every living organism. As the population increases, the demand for food also rises. Scientific techniques are therefore used to improve both the quantity and quality of food produced from crops and animals.

This chapter discusses crop improvement programmes, proper crop production management, irrigation, nutrient management, crop protection, grain storage, animal husbandry, fisheries and apiculture. These methods help farmers achieve higher productivity while ensuring sustainable use of natural resources.
