Improvement in Crop Yields

Crop yield means the amount of crop produced from a particular area of land. Scientists and farmers use modern agricultural practices to increase crop production while maintaining soil fertility and environmental balance.

🌾 What is Crop Yield?

Crop yield is the quantity of agricultural produce obtained from a field in a given period. Increasing crop yield helps meet the food requirements of the growing population and improves farmers' income.

Improvement in crop yields depends on better crop varieties, proper nutrient management, efficient irrigation, crop protection and modern farming techniques.

📚 Major Approaches to Improve Crop Yield

🌱 Crop Variety Improvement

Developing high-yielding, disease-resistant and climate-tolerant crop varieties.

🌿 Crop Production Management

Providing proper nutrients, irrigation and field management to crops.

🛡 Crop Protection Management

Protecting crops from weeds, insects, pests and diseases to reduce losses.

🌾 Factors Affecting Crop Yield

Crop production depends on several important factors. Proper management of these factors helps farmers obtain healthy crops and higher yields.

  • Good Quality Seeds: Healthy and disease-resistant seeds produce better crops.
  • Soil Fertility: Fertile soil supplies essential nutrients required for plant growth.
  • Water Supply: Timely irrigation prevents water stress and improves production.
  • Fertilisers and Manures: These provide nutrients needed for proper plant development.
  • Protection from Pests: Crops must be protected from insects, diseases and weeds.
  • Favourable Climate: Temperature, rainfall and sunlight greatly influence crop growth.

🎯 Objectives of Crop Yield Improvement

📈 Higher Production

Increase the amount of food produced per unit area.

🦠 Disease Resistance

Develop crops that resist diseases and insect attacks.

🌦 Climate Adaptation

Develop varieties that grow well under different climatic conditions.

🥦 Better Quality

Improve the nutritional value and market quality of crops.

💡 Remember

Crop yield improvement is achieved through crop variety improvement, crop production management and crop protection management.

📝 JKBOSE Exam Tip

The three major approaches to improving crop yield are among the most frequently asked theory questions in JKBOSE examinations.

📖 Summary

  • Crop yield is the amount of crop produced from a given area.
  • Better seeds and proper farming increase production.
  • Irrigation and nutrient management improve plant growth.
  • Crop protection reduces losses caused by pests and diseases.
  • Scientific farming increases both quantity and quality of food.
