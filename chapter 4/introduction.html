Introduction

Food is essential for the survival of every living organism. As the world's population continues to grow, the demand for food also increases. To meet this demand, modern scientific methods are used to improve food production while maintaining quality and protecting natural resources.

🌾 Introduction

Humans depend on plants and animals for food. Crops provide grains, fruits, vegetables, pulses and oils, while animals provide milk, eggs, meat and other useful products. Producing enough food for everyone is one of the greatest challenges faced by agriculture today.

Scientists and farmers work together to improve food resources by developing better crop varieties, increasing crop yield, protecting crops from pests and diseases, improving animal breeds and using modern farming techniques.

❓ Why is Improvement in Food Resources Necessary?

Food production must increase because the human population is growing every year. At the same time, agricultural land and natural resources are limited.

  • To provide sufficient food for the increasing population.
  • To improve the quality and nutritional value of food.
  • To reduce crop losses caused by pests and diseases.
  • To increase farmers' income.
  • To ensure sustainable use of natural resources.

🚜 Modern Methods of Improving Food Resources

To meet the increasing demand for food, scientists have developed several scientific techniques that help farmers produce more food with better quality.

🌱 Crop Variety Improvement

Developing better varieties of crops that produce higher yields, resist diseases and adapt to different climates.

💧 Crop Production Management

Using proper irrigation, fertilizers, manures and modern farming methods to increase crop production.

🛡 Crop Protection

Protecting crops from insects, weeds, fungi and diseases using scientific methods.

🐄 Animal Husbandry

Scientific care, breeding and management of animals for milk, meat, eggs and other useful products.

🐟 Fisheries

Scientific production and management of fish to meet the growing demand for protein-rich food.

🐝 Apiculture

Bee keeping for the production of honey, beeswax and better pollination of crops.

💡 Remember

Improvement in food resources means increasing both the quantity and quality of food by using scientific methods, without damaging the environment.

📝 JKBOSE Exam Tip

Questions are frequently asked about the need for improving food resources and the different methods used to increase food production. Learn these points with suitable examples.

📖 Summary

  • Food is essential for all living organisms.
  • Population growth increases the demand for food.
  • Scientific methods help improve food production.
  • Crop improvement increases yield and quality.
  • Animal husbandry, fisheries and apiculture are important food resources.
  • Sustainable farming protects natural resources for future generations.
